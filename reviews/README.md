# Reviews

Peer-review record for the working paper, per `manual.md` §3 (repository structure).

- `reviewer_reports.md` — three simulated reviewer reports (methodology, signal
  processing/physics, ML evaluation), each with a summary, major/minor concerns, and a
  decision.
- `revision_response.md` — point-by-point response mapping every reviewer concern to the
  response, the manuscript change, and the supporting artifact. Also records the citation
  verification log (Lu et al. correction, Vieira et al. preprint labelling).

These files are the authoritative review record. The copies previously kept under `ars/`
are retained there for the integrity self-audit trail; the two are kept in sync.
